The hiding history thing is kinda jank

 The difference between the hidden one and the normal one is that the url stays the same when you change pages. This means:
1. If someone was looking through your history they'd see 1 website instead of a ton
2. If they got suspicious and clicked on it, theyd see normal google (but logged out and under a different url)
3. The title of the tab can stay as "google" instead of changing with the site you go to

The only problem is that if they removed the /hidden part of the url, they'd find the main page. (If you get caught dont you dare say i made this just say you found it somewhere on google but forgot where. You'll probably get your computer taken away for a week. (I know from experience))
